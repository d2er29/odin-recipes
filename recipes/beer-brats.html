Beer Brats

Description

The brats will make your mouth water. They are super easy to make and are one of my go-tos for get togethers. They take little time and are very hands off. The end result is a very juicy and delicious brat with a subtle beer taste that enhances the flavors of the brat.

Ingredients

  • 4 cans of a mild beer (Pilzners are a good choice)
  • 1 package of raw brats

  1. Pour beers into a large pot and bring to a low boil.
  2. Add brats to beer pot and boil for 20 minutes to ensure cooking through.
  3. While brats are boiling bring grill up to temp to a medium high heat.
  4. Once brats are done boiling take out of beer and put directly on grill.
  5. Cook on grill until you get the desired grill marks. This step is personal preference since brats will be already cooked from the boil.
  6. Pull brats off of grill and put in bun with favorite condiments, or otherwise.
